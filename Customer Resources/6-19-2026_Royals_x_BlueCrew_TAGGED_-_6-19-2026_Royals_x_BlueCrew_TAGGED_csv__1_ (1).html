About the DMG Grade (Decision Making Grade)
The DMG grade evaluates a hitter's in-game decision quality — zone swing rate, chase rate, and good-eye rate — focusing entirely on the choices a hitter makes rather than what happens after contact. Because decisions are repeatable and controllable, DMG is a reliable predictor of future plate performance. A strong DMG grade means the hitter is reading pitches and making smart swing decisions, independent of whether they got a hit.
